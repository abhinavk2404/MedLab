Information Required for Diagnosis



Polyuria

Polyuria is a condition where the body urinates more than usual and passes excessive or abnormally large amounts of urine each time you urinate.

Polydipsia

Polydipsia is a medical name for the feeling of extreme thirstiness. Polydipsia is often linked to urinary conditions that cause you to urinate a lot.

Partial Paresis

Paresis involves the weakening of a muscle or group of muscles. It may also be referred to as partial or mild paralysis. Unlike paralysis, people with paresis can still move their muscles. These movements are just weaker than normal.

Alopecia

Alopecia is the medical term for hair loss. Hair loss may occur naturally or it may be related to disease or the use of certain medications.
